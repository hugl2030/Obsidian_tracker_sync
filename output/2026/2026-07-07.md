                   Long‐persistent luminescence (LPL) materials capable of storing and releasing optical energy over extended timescales are highly desirable for next‐generation photonic technologies, yet structurally stable integrated donor‐acceptor (D‐A) systems capable of day‐scale, color‐tunable LPL remain elusive.
